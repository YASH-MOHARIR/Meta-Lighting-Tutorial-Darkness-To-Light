## **üìä What Algorithms Reward**

Focus your efforts on these key signals:

* **Retention** \- Players coming back daily/weekly  
* **Visits** \- You are attracting many new users into the world  
* **Time** \- Players are staying for above average platform play sessions.   
* **Freshness** \- Regular updates and new content  
* **Social signals** \- Shares, clips, mentions outside the platform  
* **Relevance** \- Engaging with trends and community interests
